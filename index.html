About Me

I'm Leorus Studios, a passionate visual storyteller with over 8 years of experience in cinematography and photography. My journey began in the mountains of Colorado, where I first discovered my love for capturing the raw beauty of nature.

Today, I specialize in creating cinematic narratives for brands, documentaries, and creative projects. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to produce compelling visuals that resonate with audiences.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking remote trails or experimenting with new editing techniques. I believe that every frame tells a story, and I'm dedicated to telling yours with authenticity and artistry.
